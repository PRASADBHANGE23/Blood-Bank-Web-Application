Introduction

There’s no end to the benefits of donating blood for those who need it. According to the Red Cross, one donation can save as many as three lives, and someone in the India needs blood every two seconds. It turns out that donating blood doesn’t just benefit recipients. There are health benefits for donors, too, on top of the benefits that come from helping others. According to WHO, India suffers from an annual deficit of two million blood units, as ony 1% of India population donates blood each year.
Registration
In order to use this website, you need to register first. Once you are registered, you should login to access all the information available on this website.
Search
With this website, you can search for blood banks near you and contact them via the provided contact information. Admins will keep these details updated on a regular basis.
Request
Furthermore, the website allows you to request a certain blood type, and upon reviewing the information you provide on this site, our administrators will make sure that particular blood type is entered into our database as soon as possible. The information is accessible through the search section.
